Experience & Expectation

on realism, uncertainty, and collective minds.

Great plans are grounded in experience and realism — what can be reused, reapplied, and practically achieved. This is why meaning matters not only as lived experience, but also as expectation. In that second realm, uncertainty waits to be navigated and stress to be regulated.

It reveals a productive tension between the beginner's mind and the expert's mind. When both work on a hard problem, each operating from their own strength, the collaboration becomes more than the sum of its parts.
